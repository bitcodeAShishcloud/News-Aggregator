At News Aggregator, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you interact with our platform.

By using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

1. Introduction

We believe in transparency and user control. This policy explains your rights and our responsibilities regarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of News Aggregator, whether you access our Service through:

  • Web browsers (desktop and mobile)
  • Direct URL access

Our Commitment: We practice data minimization and only collect information necessary to provide you with a personalized news experience. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a personalized news experience.

Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen display name (3-30 characters)
  • Email Address: Must be a valid @gmail.com address for local registration
  • Password: Securely hashed using bcrypt algorithm (we never store plaintext passwords)
  • Security Question & Answer: Encrypted and used for account recovery
  • Account Creation Date: Timestamp of registration

Google Authentication Data

If you sign in using Google, we receive:

  • Email Address: Your verified Google email
  • Profile Name: Used to generate your username
  • Google User ID: Unique identifier for authentication
  • Authentication Token: Provided by Google's secure OAuth 2.0 APIs

Google Sign-In Security: We use Google's official Identity Services API with JWT validation. We do not have access to your Google password or any other Google account data beyond what is explicitly shared during the authentication process.

Usage Data

We may temporarily log non-personally identifiable information for security and analytics:

  • IP Address: For rate limiting and abuse prevention
  • Browser Type & Version: For compatibility and optimization
  • Device Information: Operating system and screen resolution
  • Timestamp Data: When you access the Service
  • Session Information: Login/logout times and session duration
  • User Agent: For session validation and security

User Preferences

  • Saved Articles: Articles you bookmark for later reading
  • Category Preferences: Your preferred news categories
  • UI Settings: Theme preferences and display options

What We DON'T Do: We do NOT use your data for selling or renting to third parties, targeted advertising or marketing, profiling or behavioral tracking, sharing with data brokers, or any purpose not explicitly stated in this policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Storage

  • Database: MySQL database hosted on secure servers
  • Location: Data is stored on InfinityFree hosting infrastructure
  • Encryption at Rest: Database-level encryption for sensitive data
  • Backup Policy: Regular automated backups for disaster recovery

Security Measures

  • Password Hashing: Bcrypt algorithm with cost factor 12 (irreversible)
  • Session Security: HTTPOnly and SameSite cookies to prevent XSS attacks
  • Session Regeneration: New session IDs after login to prevent fixation
  • Session Validation: IP address and User-Agent verification
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
  • SQL Injection Prevention: Prepared statements with PDO
  • XSS Protection: Input sanitization and output encoding
  • HTTPS Enforcement: Encrypted data transmission (recommended)

Security Limitations: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential, using a strong unique password, logging out from shared devices, and reporting suspicious activity immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion:

  • Immediately Deleted: Active session, saved preferences, bookmarks
  • Retained for Security: Username, email, and OAuth identifiers (to prevent abuse)
  • Retention Period: Security logs retained for 90 days
