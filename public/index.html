User Guide

Home / News Feed

Browse the latest news articles from various sources. Use the search bar and filters to find specific content.

Search & Filter

  • Search: Type keywords to find articles
  • Category Filter: Filter by Top News, World, Politics, Business, Technology, Science, Health, Sports, Entertainment
  • Source Filter: Filter by news source (sorted alphabetically)

Registration

Create an account to personalize your news experience:

  • Click "Register" in the navigation
  • Choose a username and create a secure password
  • Note: A valid @gmail.com address is required for local registration
  • Alternatively, use Sign in with Google for instant 1-click registration!

Login

Access your account securely:

  • Click "Login" in the navigation
  • Enter your username or your registered email address
  • Or use Sign in with Google for faster access
  • Your profile avatar will appear in the premium header dropdown after login

Reading Articles

  • Click on article titles or "Read Full Article" to open in new tab
  • View article images, source, and publication date
  • Category badges show article topics

Mobile Experience

The News Aggregator is fully responsive and works great on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile.
